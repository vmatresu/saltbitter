## Design Principles Applied

### 1. **Progressive Disclosure**
- Essential AI information shown immediately
- Detailed explanations available on-demand
- No overwhelming users with legal jargon upfront

### 2. **Visual Distinction**
- AI profiles: ğŸ¤– badge, distinct border color
- Human profiles: âœ“ verification badge
- Paid features: Clear pricing before purchase

### 3. **User Control**
- Granular opt-in/opt-out for each AI feature
- Easy access to settings from any AI interaction
- Reversible decisions (can change preferences anytime)

### 4. **Ethical Nudges**
- Warning when boost may not be effective
- Suggestion to improve profile before spending money
- Encouragement to practice with AI before messaging humans

### 5. **Transparent Outcomes**
- Show historical performance data for paid features
- Set realistic expectations (no false promises)
- Explain WHY certain matches are suggested

### 6. **Safety-First Design**
- Mental health resources integrated into coaching
- Daily interaction limits for anxious users
- Boundary-setting prompts before escalation to dates
