## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

1. **Consent** (Article 6(1)(a)) - For psychological assessments and AI features
2. **Contract Performance** (Article 6(1)(b)) - To provide dating services
3. **Legitimate Interests** (Article 6(1)(f)) - For service improvement and fraud prevention
4. **Legal Obligation** (Article 6(1)(c)) - To comply with legal requirements

## Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights:

### 1. Right to Access (Article 15)
Request a copy of your personal data via GET `/api/gdpr/export`

### 2. Right to Erasure (Article 17)
Request account deletion via POST `/api/gdpr/delete-account` (30-day grace period)

### 3. Right to Rectification (Article 16)
Update your profile information at any time

### 4. Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
Request limitation of processing of your data

### 5. Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
Receive your data in machine-readable format (JSON)

### 6. Right to Object (Article 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interests

### 7. Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for psychological data or AI features anytime
